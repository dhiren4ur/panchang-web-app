Integration Guide

  1. Sign up at api.prokerala.com and get your client_id and client_secret.
  2. Open jyotish-api.php and set both credentials. Do NOT send a scope parameter — that causes error 605.
  3. Upload both files to your server's jyotish/ folder.
  4. Select a city preset or enter custom coordinates, pick an endpoint, and click Get Jyotish Data.
  5. Verify the raw JSON response. Once it works, you can build your Gujarati UI on top.
